WELCOME!

Through the wonder of Christ, we are like a mosaic. A broken, shattered people redeemed and brought together to display God's beauty to one another and the world. A part of Mosaic’s heartbeat from its very beginning has been to offer a church community to Walt Disney World Cast Members, a group of people who tend to have fluctuating and intensive work schedules and live a unique, daily reality created by the demands of the hospitality industry. While this campus serves all kinds of people—both those within the world of Disney and those outside of it—we try to structure our community rhythms and gatherings with a deep understanding of the WDW Cast Member experience.
